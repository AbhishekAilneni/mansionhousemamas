MAY 16

Mamas Crush Everest by 39 Runs

MHM posted 122/5 in 15 overs and bowled out Everest Community Club for 83.

MH Mamas 122/5 (15.0 ov) beat Everest Community Club 83/8 (15.0 ov) by 39 runs.

Sai Kumar Kesana anchored the innings while the bowling unit strangled the chase from the start. The 3rd win of the season pushed MHM's win rate to 60%.

🏏 Highest team score of the season: 122/5

APR 4

1-Run Thriller — Mamas Defend 72

In the most dramatic finish of the season, MHM defended just 72 against COMILLA Victorians.

MH Mamas 72/6 (15.0 ov) beat COMILLA Victorians 71/10 (13.1 ov) by 1 run.

Nerves of steel from the bowling attack sealed a legendary win. COMILLA were bowled out just 1 run short in a match that had everyone on the edge of their seats.

🔥 Closest win of the CCC Spring 2026 season

APR 12

Clinical Chase vs Himalayan Hawks

Chasing 115, the Mamas got home in just 13.3 overs with 6 wickets in hand.

A statement win showing the batting depth of this squad. The top order fired in unison, chasing down the target with 1.3 overs to spare.

⚡ Fastest chase of the season: 13.3 overs
